This data and metadata accompanies the VegetatedCurbCuts project from the ZULE lab.

dataset: temperature-data (folder) 
In this folder, there are 40 data files. Each correspond to an individual curb cut and are labelled by the InfrastructureID. They contain the fixed-sensor temperature measurements. They all have the same format, as follows.
Rows 1-3: describe the temperature sensor model, name, and serial number. 
FORMATTED: date of data collection (yyyy-MM-dd)
DATE_TIME: time of data collection (hh:mm:ss)
Temperature: time of day (AM, PM)
Relative: air temperature (Farenheit) 
Humidity: relative humidity (%)
Heat: heat index (Farenheit) 
Index: dew point (Farenheit)
Dew: data type 

dataset: bee-data-2024.csv
This dataset describes all the bee data we collected during our 2024 field season.
InfrastructureID: individual ID assigned to each curb cut 
Date: date of sampling (dd-mm-yyyy)
Time: time when sampling began
Samplers: initials of individuals who sampled the site 
Notes: any specific notes about the site 
Temperature: temperature at the time of recording as provided by Environment and Climate Change Canada's mobile app
Wind: wind speed as provided by Environment and Climate Change Canada's mobile app (km/h)
Width: width of the curb cut (metres) 
Height: length of the curb cut (metres) 
Interactions: number of times a bee interacted with (touched) vegetation in the curb cut area

dataset: vegetative-data-2024.csv
This dataset describes the vegetative data we collected in our 2024 field season.
InfrastructureID: individual ID assigned to each curb cut
Date: date of sampling (dd-mm-yyyy)
Time: time when sampling began 
Samplers: initials of individuals who sampled the site 
Description: description of the site. if curb cut was longer than 7 m, we noted it here
Length: length of the curb cut (m). if curb cut was longer than 7 m, we measured 3.5 m in either direction from the center of the site
Width: width of the curb cut (m)
tree_species: scientific name of tree present in curb cut 
tree_DBH: DBH of tree present in the curb cut (cm)
veg_cover_per: estimated cover of all types of vegetation in the curb cut (%)
veg_spon_per: estimated cover of spontaneous vegetation in the curb cut (%)
food_per: estimated cover of human food provisioning vegetation in the curb cut (%). tree indicates that tree was food provisioning.
EQAR_per: percent cover of Equisetum arvense (%)
BAAU_per: percent cover of Baptisia australis 'Minor' (%)
HEST_yellow_per: percent cover of Hemerocallis 'Stella d'Oro' (%)
HEST_red_per: percent cover of Hemerocallis 'Stella in Red' (%)
IRVE_per: percent cover of Iris versicolor (%)
PELS_per: percent cover of Perovskia x 'Little Spire' (%)
NEFA_per: percent cover of Nepeta x Faassinii 'Six Hill Giant' (%)
SANE_per: percent cover of Salvia nemerosa 'Maybacht' (%)
THPA_per: percent cover of Thelypteris palustris (%)
JUEF_per: percent cover of Juncus effusus (%)
ASSY_per: percent cover of Asclepias syriaca (%)
ASIN_per: percent cover of Asclepias incarnata (%)
CAGR_per: percent cover of Carex grayi (%)
ARME_per: percent cover of Aronia melanocarpa 'Iroquoi Beauty' (%)
COST_per: percent cover of Cornus stolonifera 'Kelseyi' (%)
DILO_per: percent cover of Diervilla lonicera (%)
ROCS_per: percent cover of Rosa 'Carefree Sunshine' (%)
SPBE_per: percent cover of Spireae betufolia 'Tor' (%)
G1_per: percent cover of unidentified grass species 1 (%)
G2_per: percent cover of unidentified grass species 2 (%)
G3_per: percent cover of unidentified grass species 3 (%)
G4_per: percent cover of unidentified grass species 4 (%)

dataset: InfraVertes_2024.kml
This is the spatial dataset recorded in Google My Maps where we recorded the sites and assigned IDs.
Name: individual ID assigned to each curb cut
geometry: WGS 84 coordinates for each site

dataset: CR300_Series_2_seconds_jun14.dat
This is the temperature dataset provided by the mobile sensor which we used for one test run.
rows 1: describes the temperature sensor used 
rows 2-4: unformatted descriptions of the data. column names are taken from row 2.
TIMESTAMP: date/time of recording (yyyy-mm-dd hh-mm-ss)
RECORD: unique ID assigned to each data point 
BattV: battery voltage 
PTempC: air temperature (degrees Celsius) 
NMEASent(1): coordinates of data point 
NMEASent(2): coordinates of data point
Latitude_A: latitude of point A (dec deg) 
Latitude_B: latitude of point B (dec deg) 
Longitude_A: longitude of point A (dec deg)
Longitude_B: longitude of point B (dec deg)
